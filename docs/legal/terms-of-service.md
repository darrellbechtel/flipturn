## What the service does

Flip Turn aggregates publicly available swim results — primarily from results.swimming.ca and similar Swimming Canada sources — and presents them to you in a mobile app. The service:

- Looks up Swimming Canada (SNC) athletes by ID and pulls their public race history.
- Computes personal bests, recent results, and basic trends.
- Notifies you when new results appear (in future versions).

We are not affiliated with Swimming Canada, your provincial section, your club, or any meet host.
